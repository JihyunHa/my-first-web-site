Brown Is Beautiful

word by Supriya Kellar, picture by Noor Sofi

Brown is beautiful. A wild mustang in a vast expanse. Shimmering mane calling for a second glance. Tiny ants lifting a weight. Rumbling train transporting freight.

Brown is stable. Twisted roots tunneling deep. Through wind and rain, the tree will keep.

Brown is nurturing. Kindling logs fueling a hungry fire. Dark soil helping plants grow higher.

Brown is brave. Mother bear protecting her young. Countless heroes, all unsung.

Brown is soaring. A mountain reaching for the sky. An eagle, wings stretched up high.

Brown is kind. A crooked hollow, home to a bird. A sheepdog watching over its herd.

Brown is wise. A wrinkled maple leaf that's seen. Spring, winter, and in between.

Brown is wonder. A stretch of canyon, ever so grand. Stripes and swirls,as if painted by hand.

Brown is curious. A tiny beak peeking in a ravine. A winding path, its end unseen.

Brown is inspiring. A raft defying churning waves. Nature's art decorating caves.

Brown is love. A cozy nest woven with care. Good news for the family to share.

Brown is potential. A new life crying out. A warm seed about to sprout.

Brown is beautiful.

Brown is me.
